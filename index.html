Professional Summary

I am a Software Architect with over 18 years of experience designing, modernizing, and delivering enterprise platforms built on .NET and Microsoft Azure. My work focuses on transforming complex legacy systems into cloud-native, API-first architectures that scale reliably and support modern business needs.

Throughout my career, I have led technical modernization initiatives across industries including insurance, healthcare, retail, telecommunications, and public sector operations. I specialize in moving organizations from monolithic on-premises infrastructure to microservices-based systems running on Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS), with comprehensive CI/CD automation and observability built in from day one.

More recently, I have integrated practical AI capabilities into enterprise systems—not as standalone experiments, but as embedded features that enhance existing workflows. This includes implementing Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architectures, agentic AI workflows, and LLM-powered automation within governed, secure environments.

I approach every engagement with a focus on business outcomes: improving system performance, reducing operational costs, accelerating time-to-market, and building platforms that teams can maintain and evolve confidently. Whether advising on architecture strategy, hands-on system design, or mentoring engineering teams, my goal is to deliver tangible, lasting value.

I work with organizations that recognize technology as a strategic enabler and are ready to invest in modern, sustainable solutions that support long-term growth.

Responsible AI & Modern Architecture

AI is most valuable when it augments existing workflows rather than replacing them. My approach integrates large language models and intelligent automation into enterprise systems as practical, governed capabilities—not experimental features.

Embedded AI, Not Bolted-On

LLM capabilities are integrated directly into existing business processes—document analysis, customer support routing, data enrichment—within secure, auditable frameworks.

RAG & Agentic Workflows

Retrieval-Augmented Generation architectures ground AI responses in your organization's data. Agentic workflows automate multi-step tasks with human oversight at decision points.

Model Context Protocol (MCP)

Implement standardized context-sharing patterns so AI systems access enterprise knowledge securely, with proper access controls and version tracking.

Governance & Observability

Every AI interaction is logged, monitored, and auditable. Costs are tracked, quality is measured, and fallback mechanisms ensure reliability when models underperform.
